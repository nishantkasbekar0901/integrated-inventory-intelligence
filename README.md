## Overview

Integrated Inventory Intelligence (I³) is an academic product design prototype developed as part of IT Product Design coursework.

The project explores how organizations can improve inventory visibility, supplier monitoring, stock planning, and operational decision-making through a centralized analytics-driven platform.

The prototype demonstrates workflow design, operational intelligence concepts, dashboard architecture, and inventory management processes.

---

## Problem Statement

Organizations frequently face challenges such as:

- Inventory shortages
- Overstocking
- Expiring stock
- Delayed supplier deliveries
- Limited operational visibility
- Manual inventory tracking

These challenges can increase operational costs and impact business performance.

I³ aims to provide a centralized view of inventory operations to support informed decision-making.

## Disclaimer

This project is an academic product design prototype created for educational purposes.

The application uses simulated inventory data and does not connect to live ERP systems, databases, warehouse management systems, supplier networks, or external APIs.

The prototype is intended to demonstrate workflow concepts, operational dashboards, and inventory management processes rather than production-grade functionality.
